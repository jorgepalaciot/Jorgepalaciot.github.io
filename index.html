Case studies

Problems I've actually solved.

Real projects, real constraints, real results. Client names anonymised where required.

01
Facilities / Real estate operations

Building an elevator maintenance intelligence system from zero data

A Lima residential building had no maintenance history and was repeatedly presented with costly upgrades by its service provider. With no existing data to work from, I designed and implemented a failure log system, tracked every incident and intervention over time, and applied pattern analysis to the records.

The finding: 95% of mechanical failures occurred within five days of a technician visit — a pattern invisible without the log, and one that fundamentally changed the negotiating position with the provider.

Data logging from scratch Failure pattern analysis Vendor negotiation Preventive maintenance redesign
30% reduction in unplanned maintenance costs
2019–2021 Lima, Peru
02
Hospitality / Service operations

Redesigning hotel front desk flow to eliminate hidden delays

Three Lima hotels were experiencing check-in delays averaging 8 minutes during peak hours. Observation and process mapping revealed three steps that existed for historical reasons but added no value to the guest or the operation — including a redundant paper verification that duplicated a digital check already in place.

After mapping the current state, identifying waste, and piloting the redesigned flow, average check-in time dropped to 3 minutes without any new technology investment.

Value stream mapping Lean waste analysis Process redesign Staff workflow training
63% reduction in average check-in time
2017–2018 Lima, Peru
03
Business consulting / Supply chain

Process documentation and waste mapping for SME logistics clients

Multiple small and mid-sized businesses in Lima engaged for process mapping consultations focused on logistics and internal operations. In each case, the starting point was the same: informal processes that existed in people's heads but not on paper, making improvement impossible to measure or sustain.

Deliverables included BPMN process maps, waste identification reports, and prioritised improvement roadmaps. Several clients implemented recommendations that reduced internal process steps by 20–40%.

BPMN mapping Stakeholder interviews Lean Six Sigma tools Improvement roadmaps
20–40% reduction in internal process steps
2016–present Lima, Peru
